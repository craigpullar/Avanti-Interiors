About us

At Avanti Interiors we specialise in office partitions and suspended ceilings. We can assist in your design and offer advice if required. We offer a complete service that can include mezzanine floors, air conditioning, heating and plumbing, electrics and data, decoration, flooring, fire proofing, soundproofing and insulation and office furniture.

We have had many years experience working for many large companies to very small companies. All our contractors are bona fide, long established, qualified Tradesmen.
